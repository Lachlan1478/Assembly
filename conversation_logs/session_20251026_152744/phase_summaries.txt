======================================================================
PHASE SUMMARIES
======================================================================

Phase: IDEATION
----------------------------------------------------------------------
During the ideation phase, one startup idea, VisionaryHub, was generated. VisionaryHub is a virtual collaboration platform leveraging AI to enhance creativity and innovation within remote-first companies. The key decision was to focus on addressing the needs of remote work productivity and team collaboration for distributed teams, emphasizing visual stimulation and interactive tools for brainstorming and project planning.

Phase: DECISION
----------------------------------------------------------------------
During the decision phase of the meeting, the team consolidated feedback and proposed startup ideas in JSON format. Two startup ideas were presented - "TeamFlow" and "HarmonyHub". The team will now work on further refining these ideas to align with the given inspiration around remote work productivity and team collaboration for remote-first companies and distributed teams.
